Acceptable Forms of Identification

Every signer must be identified through one of the forms of ID listed in California Code 1185[b]

A form of identification is mandatory for every notary. This requirement is in place to ensure the verification of your identity and to prevent fraudulent activities. All names on the identification forms match the name in the document being notarized. Your cards or passports must not be expired, or in bad shape. If you have any further inquiries or need additional information, please don't hesitate to contact us.



State ID's
  • Identification (ID) card
  • California Driver's License (CDL)
  • U.S. military ID
  • State ID or Driver's License
U.S. Passports
  • U.S. Passport
  • U.S. Passport Card
